## About Pavan Kumar Appannagari

Pavan Kumar Appannagari is a software engineer focused on building reliable software systems across platforms — currently in mobile, and increasingly in applied AI and system performance.

He writes about how software behaves, why systems drift over time, and how architecture influences correctness more than implementation.

## Areas of Interest

- Mobile architecture (iOS & Android)
- Kotlin Multiplatform and cross-platform system design
- Performance engineering
- Applied AI in real software systems
- Data engineering and validation correctness

This site is a collection of technical notes exploring how software systems make decisions — and how to make those decisions consistent.

## Themes You’ll See Often

A recurring idea across many posts:

> Feature parity is rarely a testing problem.  
> It is usually an architectural one.
